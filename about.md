# About Me

![Dr. Ahmed Abide Tadesse](/assets/images/profile.jpg){: style="width:220px;border-radius:50%;float:right;margin-left:25px;margin-bottom:20px;" }

## Biography

I am **Dr. Ahmed Abide Tadesse**, an Assistant Professor of Industrial Engineering at **Bahir Dar University**, Ethiopia, and Chair of the **Industrial Design and Management Science (IDMS)**. My academic and professional work focuses on advancing intelligent manufacturing through interdisciplinary research in Human–Robot Collaboration, Artificial Intelligence, Smart Manufacturing, Human Factors Engineering, and Industry 5.0.

I hold dual PhD degrees in **Industrial Management** from National Taiwan University of Science and Technology (NTUST) and **Industrial & Systems Engineering** from Bahir Dar University. My research integrates artificial intelligence, robotics, augmented reality, computer vision, and system modeling to develop intelligent and human-centered manufacturing systems.
