# Dr. Ahmed Abide Tadesse

Assistant Professor of Industrial Engineering  
Chair Holder, Industrial Design and Management Science (IDMS) Chair  
Bahir Dar Institute of Technology, Bahir Dar University, Ethiopia

## Academic Background

I received my PhD in Industrial Management from National Taiwan University of Science and Technology (NTUST) in 2023 and my PhD in Industrial and Systems Engineering from Bahir Dar University.

My research focuses on human-centered intelligent manufacturing systems, Industry 5.0, and advanced human-robot collaboration technologies.

## Research Interests

- Human-Robot Collaboration (HRC)
- Industry 5.0 and Smart Manufacturing
- Augmented Reality (AR) in Manufacturing
- Human Factors Engineering
- System Modeling and Simulation
- Robotics and Intelligent Manufacturing Systems
- Hidden Semi-Markov Models (HSMM)
