## Engineering Intelligent Manufacturing Through Human-Centered Artificial Intelligence

Welcome to my academic website.

I am **Dr. Ahmed Abide Tadesse**, an Assistant Professor of Industrial Engineering at Bahir Dar University and Chair of the Industrial Design and Management Science (IDMS). My research focuses on developing intelligent manufacturing systems by integrating artificial intelligence, robotics, augmented reality, and human-centered engineering to support Industry 5.0.

# Selected Research Areas

## Human–Robot Collaboration

Developing adaptive collaboration models for intelligent manufacturing using Hidden Semi-Markov Models, computer vision, and augmented reality.

## Smart Manufacturing

Applying AI, optimization, and cyber-physical systems to improve industrial productivity.

## Human Factors Engineering

Investigating cognitive workload, ergonomics, and human-centered system design.

## System Modeling & Simulation

Using simulation and optimization tools to model manufacturing systems and support decision-making.
