# Publications

My research publications focus on Human-Robot Collaboration, Augmented Reality, Intelligent Manufacturing Systems, Industry 5.0, and Human Factors Engineering.

## Journal Publications

### 1. Multi-modal Empowerment for Human-Robot Collaboration Team: A Hidden Semi-Markov Model and an Augmented Reality-Based Collaborative Assembly System

**Year:** 2022  

**Research areas:**
- Human-Robot Collaboration
- Hidden Semi-Markov Models
- Augmented Reality
- Collaborative Assembly Systems
- Human Factors Engineering

**Summary:**  
This research proposes a multimodal empowerment framework for human-robot collaboration by integrating human state recognition, hidden semi-Markov modeling, and augmented reality-based interaction to improve collaborative assembly performance.

### 2. Intelligent Task Allocation for Human-Robot Collaboration Using Fuzzy Logic Approaches

**Year:** 2023  

**Research areas:**
